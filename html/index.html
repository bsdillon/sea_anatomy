  1. Sponges have the remarkable ability to grow and reconstitute themselves even when damaged or cut into small pieces. Let's consider a hypothetical species of sponge that grows at a rate of 20.6% by volume per year. A sponge starts with a volume of 500 cubic centimeters.
    • How much will it grow in 1 year?
    • In 5 years?
    • In 10 years?
  2. After the 500 cubic centimeter sponge is reduced to fine particles, it begins to reconstitute itself at a rate of 10% of its volume per day.
    • How many days will it take for the sponge to reconstitute itself completely?
